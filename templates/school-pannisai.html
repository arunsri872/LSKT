PANNISAI AT LSKTSeparate classes are available for both youngsters and elders to learn all types of Tamil hymns such as thevaram, thirupuranam, thiruvasakam and much more. Classes are done inside the temple to learn in front of the Lord. There are lots of opportunities to perform such as singing during festivals held at the temple and other temples around the UK. You can also choose to take part in competitions such as the one held by the Federation of Saiva (Hindu) Temples UK. Before school begins, all Hindu students are also encouraged to take part in a prayer where Siva Puranam is lead by the Othuvar Moorthy.
