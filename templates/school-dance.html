TEACHING BHARATHANATYAMBharatanatyam is one of the most divine dance forms of India. Bharatanatyam’s statues poses, geometric lines, detailed hand and eye movements and complex foot rhythms around a deeply spiritual core. The rhythmic use of time in the music and steps is a sacred language that has survived life’s changes through millennia.

SYLABUSThe Bharathanatyam Department follows the Oriental Fine Arts Academy of London (OFAAL) Curriculum. This is an exam board that provides the OFQUAL qualified RSL Award for extra curricular and musical activities. Student are prepared to take the exam to the next grade at their own speed and parental discretion all the way up to Grade 8 (Diploma) where the students graduate. Please use curriculum info below.

All pupils are given the opportunity to perform at the Lewisham Sivan Kovil annual programmes, charity events and UK wide cultural conferences.
Teachers can be contacted at the end of the school day, via the school co-ordinator Sharmilla or at our termly coffee mornings. You can also send an email at: londonsivant@gmail.com
