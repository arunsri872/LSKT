HEALTH AND SAFETY AT SCHOOLSafeguarding is LSKT School's top priority. To provide you with piece of mind and to make sure your child is safe, the school takes tremendous steps to keep child safety our top priority.
Children are escorted by staff members from parents upstairs to the classrooms and backdown again until they are picked up. This is to ensure nobody gets lost or left behind.

If a child takes more than one subject, they are escorted by staff to their next class.
LSKT School has an onsite qualified first aid and safety co-ordinator.

Teachers have been DBS checked to ensure you get the best.
