SYLABUSThe Keyboard Carnatic Music Department follows the Oriental Fine Arts Academy of London (OFAAL) Curriculum. This is an exam board that provides the OFQUAL qualified RSL Award for extra curricular and musical activities. Student are prepared to take the exam to the next grade at their own speed and parental discretion all the way up to Grade 8 (Diploma) where the students graduate. Please use curriculum info below.

All pupils are given the opportunity to perform at the Lewisham Sivan Kovil annual programmes, charity events and UK wide cultural conferences.
